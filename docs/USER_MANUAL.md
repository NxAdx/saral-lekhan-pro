# User Manual - सरल लेखन (Saral Lekhan)

Welcome to **सरल लेखन**, a premium, tactile notes application designed for a beautiful and simple writing experience.

## 1. Getting Started
When you open the app, you'll see your list of notes. If it's your first time, the screen will be empty.

### Creating a Note
- Tap the large **terracotta floating button (+)** at the bottom right.
- This will open the **New Note** screen.

## 2. The Editor
The editor is where you'll spend most of your time. It supports rich text formatting and smart tools.

### Toolbar Controls
- **B, I, U**: Bold, Italic, Underline.
- **H1, H2**: Header styles.
- **Lists**: Toggle bulleted or numbered lists.
- **Blockquote**: For highlighting important text.
- **। and ॥**: Specific Hindi punctuation marks for easy access.

### Word & Character Count
- Located at the bottom bar of the editor to help you track your progress.

### Spark AI Tools (Premium)
If you have a Gemini API key configured in Settings, you can use:
- **Smart Title**: Generates a title based on your note content.
- **Summarize**: Creates a brief summary of your note.
- **Format Note**: Automatically cleans up and formats your text.
- **Write for Me**: Enter a prompt, and the AI will generate text directly in your note.

### Saving & Exiting
- **Auto-save**: If enabled in settings, your note is saved as you type.
- **Done**: Tap 'Done' at the top right to save and return to the home screen.
- **Back**: If Auto-save is off and you have unsaved changes, the app will ask if you want to save or discard them.

## 3. Organizing Notes
### Tags
- You can add a single tag to any note (e.g., #विचार, #कविता, #English).
- On the home screen, tap the tag pills at the top to filter your notes.

### Search
- Use the search bar at the top to find notes by their title or content.

### Pinned Notes
- Long-press a note on the home screen or toggle the "Pinned" status in the editor (if applicable) to keep important notes at the top.

## 4. Backup & Sync (Google Drive)
Keep your notes safe by syncing them with your Google account.
- Go to **Settings > Data & Storage**.
- Sign in with Google.
- Tap **Backup** to upload your database to your private Google Drive app folder.
- Tap **Restore** to download and merge your data on a new device.

## 5. Security (Biometric Lock)
- Go to **Settings > Security**.
- Enable **Biometric Lock** to protect your notes with fingerprint or face ID.

## 6. Personalization
### Themes
- Choose from various premium color palettes (Nord, Pitch, Classic, etc.) in **Settings > Appearance**.

### Typography
- Adjust the **Text Size** using the Material 3 slider.
- Change the **App Font** to your preference (Poppins, Noto Sans, Baloo 2, etc.).
