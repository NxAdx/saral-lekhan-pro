Saral Lekhan (सरल लेखन) is a privacy-first note-taking app for writers, students,
and anyone who wants a calm place to think and write.

Key Features:

📝 Rich Text Editor
Full formatting support with bold, italic, underline, headings (H1/H2),
blockquotes, bullet lists, numbered lists, checkboxes, code blocks,
horizontal rules, links, and inline images.

✨ Spark AI (Optional)
Bring your own Gemini API key to unlock AI-powered writing tools:
• Smart title generation from your content
• Note summarization
• Auto-formatting and cleanup
• Custom AI prompts

Spark AI is disabled until you manually add your own Gemini API key.

🎨 Themes
Choose from multiple handcrafted color themes with automatic light and dark
mode support.

🔒 Privacy First
• No accounts required
• No tracking or analytics
• No ads
• All notes stay stored locally on your device
• Optional biometric lock screen

🌐 Multi-Language Support
Full interface in English, Hindi (हिंदी), and more, including Hindi punctuation
support in the editor toolbar.

📤 Export, Backup, and Restore
Export notes as beautifully formatted PDFs and import or export local database
backups when you need them.

🏷️ Organization Tools
Use tags, trash recovery, and find-and-replace tools to keep long writing
projects under control.

This app is free and open-source software published under the MIT License.
The core app works locally on-device. The optional Spark AI feature uses the
proprietary Gemini service only after you enable it with your own API key.
